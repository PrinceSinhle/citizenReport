Welcome To Witbank Community Service

A platform for Witbank residents to report community issues and track service delivery progress.

How To Report a Community Issue

1. Login or Register

Create an account or login so your complaint can be linked to your profile.

2. Report an Issue

Choose the issue type, select your Witbank location, and describe the problem clearly.

3. Track Progress

View your dashboard to see whether your complaint is submitted, in progress, or resolved.

4. Give Feedback

Share comments about the service and help improve community response.
